Typography


Open Sans is the base font type-face making up the core of the design system. The font was chosen not only because of its readability but also because of the elegance that it offers, making it the ideal one that blends in both the imaginative and pragmatic boundary. Its versatility within weight—Light, Regular, Semibold, and Bold—and the ability to be adapted to one’s taste in design work best for every scenario, from prominent headlines to subtle body text.
