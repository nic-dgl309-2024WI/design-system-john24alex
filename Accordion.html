Accordion


The accordion helps to organize the information being shown in large quantity without making a user exhausted or by reducing the beauty of the website (UX) aesthetic design.


Accordion Component

Outback Nursery began in 1998-1999 when our parents retired to the island from Aldergrove. They asked if we'd be interested in joining them and then purchased this heavily treed property. We began clearing some trees and settled into country life trying our hand at raising some turkeys and pigs as well as growing some hedging and conifers. We joined the Farmer's Market, then as our inventory expanded we began running a spec truck down island stopping here and there to show our quality product to potential customers.

our mission is to cultivate joy, sustainability, and growth through the timeless art of gardening. We are dedicated to nurturing a deep connection between people and the planet by providing an exceptional selection of high-quality plants, expert gardening advice, and unparalleled customer service. Our goal is to inspire gardeners of all levels to create beautiful, thriving outdoor spaces that benefit the environment, enhance well-being, and foster community. By embracing innovation, education, and a deep respect for nature, we commit to being stewards of the earth and enablers of dreams, one garden at a time."

Outback Nursery is one of the largest garden centres on Vancouver Island. Our nursery is situated on a 6 acre property and offers 5 overflowing greenhouses (30x170ft); almost 23,000 square feet. Little by little the property was cleared and one by one the greenhouses and irrigation zones increased to what you see today. In the beginning we were limited to wholesale and landscape clientele, then somewhere along the way interest in our nursery grew and we opened to the public.
