Breadcrumbs


breadcrumbs are a user-friendly navigation method that leads the users by a supporting points. The guide is essentially based on an understanding of the current position within the website structure and requires moving backwards and forwards among the website's various sections easily.


Breadcrumbs

  • Home
  • Category
  • Current Page


            <div class="breadcrumb-container bg-primary-background">
                <nav aria-label="Breadcrumb">
                    <ul class="breadcrumb">
                        <li><a href="#">Home</a></li>
                        <li><a href="#">Category</a></li>
                        <li>Current Page</li>
                    </ul>
                </nav>
            </div>
