Color Swatches


Nature is the main source inspiring these colors. An earthy palette is created with characteristics on a gardening website a natural theme. The brown and oak colour indicates the soil and the sand - elements which are the fundamental parts of the process of gardening. The grey might represent stone, rocks, but something similar to them is almost elemental for ordinary natural sights.
